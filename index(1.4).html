Overview

Post Malone is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. Known for his variegated vocals, Malone has gained acclaim for blending genres and subgenres of hip hop, pop, R&B, and trap.

Career

Post Malone has had many ups and downs in his career but some of his most noteable victories include, the release of the album "BeerBongs and Bentleys", and the release of his third number one single "Sunflower" featuring Swae Lee
